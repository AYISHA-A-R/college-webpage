LEAD College Placement Assistance

LEAD College is one of the premier institutions which ensures 100% placement assistance for the students. The stand-alone MBA College has their own placement club and is handled by the students itself under the guidelines of placement officer Mr. Ajay J. Every year we handhold our students and guide them through the entire process of transformation from the campus to the corporate.

Our facilitators provide a wholesome training program with the intend to equip the students with the skills required to impress recruiters. We organize evening classes for group discussions, aptitude, news analysis, resume writing, one on one sessions on interview handling and skype.
